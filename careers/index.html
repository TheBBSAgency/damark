Why Work Here

What Working at Damark Looks Like

Damark has been running the same way for thirty years: do the work right, treat people straight, and build something worth coming back to every day.

Hours that give you your day back

Monday through Friday, 6:00 AM to 2:30 PM. You’re done before most people are thinking about leaving. Go fish. Coach your kid’s game. Actually use the rest of your day.

A team that works together

Everybody here knows each other. When someone needs help, they get it. No politics, no cliques, no one looking over your shoulder. You come in, do good work, and you’re treated like an adult.

Work that keeps you sharp

Small batch, production runs, prototypes, fixtures, and more. You won’t be doing the same operation on the same part for the next ten years. If you like solving problems, this is the right kind of shop.

Room to grow

We’ve taken people with limited experience and developed them into solid machinists. If you’re willing to learn and you show up ready to work, we’ll invest in you.

Who Fits Here

Who We're Looking For

You’re mechanically inclined

You understand how things go together and come apart. You think in three dimensions. You don’t need everything spelled out for you.

You’re self-directed

We’ll give you the job. We trust you to get it done. We’re not going to stand over you, but we expect you to come back when something doesn’t look right.

You take quality personally

A bad part leaving this shop reflects on all of us. We’re looking for people who care about that.

You work well with others

This is a small team. Attitude matters. We’d rather have someone with less experience and the right mindset than a highly skilled machinist who makes everyone miserable.
