Quality

How We Handle Quality

Quality at Damark is not a department. It's how the work gets done. Every job is built to be repeatable, documented, and verified — so the parts you get meet the standards your customers require.

01

Documented routings

Every job runs through documented in-process routings and machine setup sheets, so the work is repeatable from the first part to the last.

02

Calibrated inspection

We use calibrated gauges and inspection equipment, including optical comparator measurement, to verify parts at setup and during production runs.

03

ISO-compliant processes

Our processes are ISO compliant. We support first sample inspection reports and documentation that meets your customers’ supplier quality requirements.

04

In-process checks

Our machinists perform in-process checks throughout the job — not just at the end — so problems are caught while they’re still cheap to fix.
